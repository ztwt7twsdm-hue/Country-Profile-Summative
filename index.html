Challenges

  • Trade deficit

Culture

Do's

  1. Be punctual and polite
  2. Use formal greetings in business settings
  3. Respect Macedonian language requirements

Don'ts

  1. Do not ignore bilingual labeling laws
  2. Do not rush relationship-building
  3. Do not use informal language too quickly in business

Cultural Determinants

History: Greek, Roman, Ottoman, and Yugoslav influences

Geography: Mountainous Balkan landscape and regional crossroads

Religion: Mainly Christianity, with a significant Muslim population
